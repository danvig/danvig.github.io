"Someone has taken their love of scary movies one step too far."

A year after Sidney's mom is murdered, more murders start to occur. She begins to suspect if these murders are related and tries to find the killer as everyone seems to be a suspect.

FUN FACTS

  • The original title of the film was Scary Movie. This wound up being the title of the future Scream parody film.
  • The film was inspired by real stories such as The Gainesville Ripper.
  • There was a major bidding war for the script, resulting in Dimension Films buying it for $400,000.
  • Drew Barrymore was set to play Sidney Prescott, but changed her mind and decided she wanted to play Casey instead.
    • Killing Drew off in the first 10 minutes of the film made the opening scene one of the most iconic movie scenes in history.
  • The "Ghost Face" mask was found during a location scout, however the filmmakers originally had a replica made when they didn't have the rights to use it.
  • Linda Blair, an iconic horror queen, has a brief cameo in the film.
  • A high school allowed production to film there but then changed their mind, causing Wes Craven to leave a message for them in the ending credits.
  • Director Wes Craven makes a cameo as "Fred the Janitor". He wears a Freddy Krueger sweater from A Nightmare on Elm Street, which Craven also directed.
  • The final act of the film took over twenty days to film.
  • The film was originally given an NC-17 rating, meaning a lot of gore had to be cut.
  • Roger L. Jackson was never allowed to meet the cast as Craven wanted his voice to remain a mystery for the cast.
  • Rose McGowan dyed her hair blonde so that she could secure the role of Tatum.
  • Neve Campbell and Matthew Lillard wound up dating for two years after production.
