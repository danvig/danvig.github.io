About Us

"We simplify complex legal issues with personalised, clear, and effective solutions."

Established in 1988, Spencer Law Partners has been a cornerstone of Melbourne's legal community for over three decades. Our firm is located in the heart of Melbourne's Central Business District, within the iconic Mondriane Tower on Spencer Street—formerly known as Spencer House. This historic location reflects our deep roots in Melbourne’s rich heritage and commitment to the city.

We specialise in Property Law, Finance, Leasing, Commercial Litigation, Family Law, and Wills and Estates. As a general practice law firm, our team is well-equipped to offer tailored advice across a wide range of legal matters, ensuring our clients receive reliable, practical, and comprehensive solutions to their unique challenges.

Spencer Law Partners pride ourselves on our client-centric philosophy. For over 35 years, we have worked collaboratively with our clients to understand their circumstances, provide clear and actionable insights, and deliver high-quality legal representation. Our approach is built on fostering long-term relationships, honouring commitments, and offering a personal touch that sets us apart.

Our reputation for excellence has been reinforced by our membership in the Law Institute of Victoria, reflecting our adherence to the highest standards of professionalism and ethics. We are particularly experienced in simplifying complex legal issues, helping clients navigate their cases with clarity and confidence. Our services are designed not just to meet expectations but to exceed them.

At Spencer Law Partners, we combine decades of experience with a forward-thinking approach, ensuring our clients are equipped to handle both present and future challenges. Let us help you achieve clarity, confidence, and success in your legal journey.

What We Offer

Spencer Law Partners is a general law practice that offers services to our clients in several diverse areas. Our areas of practice include:

Property Law • Finance • Leasing • Commercial Litigation • Family Law • Wills and Estates

Commercial & Corporate

Our services in commercial and corporate-related matters include representing individuals and corporations in sales and purchases of businesses, drafting and negotiating contracts and commercial agreements, joint venture agreements, trusts, partnerships, and franchise law. We also have extensive experience in bankruptcy and insolvency, commercial leasing, and litigation.

Property

We advise clients with respect to all property-related matters. Our services include sales and purchases of real estate, property developments, advising clients with respect to major projects, subdivisions, owners corporations, adverse possession claims, and comprehensive experience in planning, construction, and litigation.

Banking & Finance

Our services in banking and finance-related matters include advising both borrowers and lenders with respect to mortgages and other securities, managing banking and finance-related issues, debt recovery, and litigation concerning the enforcement of mortgages and other securities.

Wills & Estates

We are particularly experienced in probate matters and our services include administering deceased estates, preparing wills and codicils, obtaining probate and letters of administration, and litigation concerning contested claims against deceased estates. We assist clients in managing deceased estates efficiently.
