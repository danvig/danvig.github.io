About Us

The Parker-Nichols Hotel was founded in 2013 by two brothers, Drake Parker and Josh Nichols.


Nestled against the sunny backdrop of San Diego, California, the motel provides 20 exclusive and luxurious rooms of differing capacities to its customers. Tourists and locals come far and wide to enjoy the comforts of the Parker-Nichols Motel, which is why we've stayed in business in such a difficult industry.


Relax and enjoy the sunny surroundings of San Diego, as we are located right in the heart of the City. We are a family owned and operated business that provides a stay our customers will never forget. All our rooms come equipped with extra large beds, high speed internet access, and state-of-the-art amenities, ensuring your stay is as relaxing as possible.


Whether you are a solo explorer or part of a group of adventurers, we are all about ensuring you have a memorable and relaxing visit.
